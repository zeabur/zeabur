# Sales Partner

The Sales Partner program is for **enterprise consultants, system integrators, and technology consulting firms** who help enterprise clients adopt Zeabur and earn recurring commissions for as long as the client continues to pay.

## When It Applies

The Sales Partner program applies in the following scenarios:

- **Single contract value exceeds US$1,000**
- **Institutional bulk purchase** (including Vouchers, invoicing required, contract required)

If the amount is below the threshold and the user pays individually through self-service, the [Referral Program](/rewards/referral) automatic flow applies instead.

<Callout type="info">
A single transaction can only go through the referral code or Sales Partner path — **they do not stack**.
</Callout>

## Commission Model

Sales Partners earn a **15% recurring commission**:

- For each client you bring in, you receive **15%** of their monthly payment as commission.
- As long as the client continues to use and pay for Zeabur, the commission keeps flowing.
- Commissions are settled monthly and paid out as Zeabur Credits or other agreed methods.

### Example

You help an enterprise adopt Zeabur, and the client pays US$2,000 per month. You earn US$2,000 × 15% = **US$300** per month for as long as the client remains active.

## Sales Partner vs. Referral Code

| Aspect | Referral Code | Sales Partner |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Threshold | None | Single deal > US$1,000 or institutional purchase |
| Trigger | Automatic (user self-service payment) | Manual (contract signing) |
| Reward model | Commission % × payment amount (one-time) | 15% recurring commission (ongoing) |
| Target audience | Any user | Enterprise consultants / Integrators |

<Callout type="warning">
KOL Override (admin-configured custom referral rates) and Sales Partner should not be applied to the same account simultaneously to avoid double-benefit conflicts.
</Callout>

## Partnership Process

1. **Contact Zeabur** — Reach out via the community forum or email to express interest, describing your client background and estimated contract scale.
2. **Confirm partnership** — The Zeabur team reviews and confirms the partnership terms with you.
3. **Client onboarding** — Help the client evaluate, test, and go live on Zeabur.
4. **Contract signing** — The client signs a contract with Zeabur and completes the first month's payment.
5. **Start earning commissions** — Once the client's first payment succeeds, you receive 15% of their monthly payments on an ongoing basis.

## Ideal Scenarios

- **Enterprise cloud migration** — Help businesses migrate existing services to Zeabur.
- **Institutional procurement** — Educational institutions, government agencies, and other organizations purchasing Zeabur services in bulk.
- **Technical consulting** — Recommend and onboard Zeabur as the deployment platform for your clients.
