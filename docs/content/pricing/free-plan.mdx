# Free Plan

The Free Plan is Zeabur's free tier, designed for personal exploration and learning. No credit card required — just sign up and start deploying.

## Included Resources

| Resource | Limit |
| --- | --- |
| Projects | 1 |
| Services | Up to 2 per project |
| Deployment regions | Shared cluster regions |
| Free credits | Reset monthly (see [Free Quota](/subscription/free-quota)) |
| Custom domains | Not supported |
| Persistent storage | Not supported |

### Service Rates

The Free Plan uses the same rates as paid plans, deducted from free credits:

- **vCPU** — Free ($0.0 / vCPU-minute)
- **Memory** — US$0.00025 / GB-minute (~US$10.8 / GB-month)
- **Network egress** — US$0.1 / GB
- **Persistent storage** — US$0.2 / GB-month

## Limitations

- **Auto-sleep** — Services on the Free Plan automatically sleep after a period of inactivity. They wake up on the next incoming request, which may cause a few seconds of cold-start latency.
- **No persistent storage** — Cannot deploy services that require persistent volumes (e.g., databases).
- **No SLA** — The free plan does not include a Service Level Agreement.
- **Credits exhausted** — When monthly free credits run out, services are suspended until the next month's reset.

<Callout type="info">
Services on the Free Plan auto-sleep when idle to conserve resources. If you need services to run continuously, consider upgrading to the [Dev Plan](/pricing/dev-plan).
</Callout>

## Upgrade Path

When your needs exceed the Free Plan, you can upgrade to a paid plan:

- **[Dev Plan](/pricing/dev-plan)** — US$5/month, ideal for individual developers with production projects. Includes custom domains, persistent storage, and unlimited projects.
- **[Team Plan](/pricing/team-plan)** — Usage-based billing, designed for team collaboration with member management and invoicing support.
- **Enterprise** — Custom plans with dedicated support and SLAs — [contact us](https://zeabur.com/forum) for details.

Visit [zeabur.com/pricing](https://zeabur.com/pricing) to compare all plans.
