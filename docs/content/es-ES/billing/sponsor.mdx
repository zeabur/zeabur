# Sponsor

## What is a Sponsorship?

A sponsorship from Zeabur offers the following benefits:

- One month free of Developer Plan for all contributors.

- Credits for contributors, varying based on project scale and individual contributions.

## Sponsorship Criteria

- The project must be non-commercial and fully open source.

- It is required to display a Zeabur banner at the footer of the project's website and in the `README.md` file.

You can find the resources in [the referral page](https://dash.zeabur.com/account/referral) in our dashboard.

[![Deploy on Zeabur](https://zeabur.com/deployed-on-zeabur-dark.svg)](https://dash.zeabur.com/account/referral)

## How to Apply for Sponsorship

- Sponsorship is open to any project that fulfills the above criteria.

- To apply, follow these steps:

    - Ensure your project meets all the listed criteria.

    - Email contact@zeabur.com with confirmation of the criteria adherence and a brief explanation of why your project needs sponsorship.​​
