# Plans

Zeabur offers five subscription plans — **Free**, **Dev**, **Pro**, **Team**, and **Enterprise** — designed to grow with you from solo experiments to company-wide deployments.

For full feature comparison and the latest pricing, visit [zeabur.com/pricing](https://zeabur.com/pricing). This page focuses on the audience and supported payment methods of each plan.

![Plans](/billing/plans/overview.png)

## Free Plan

By default, every Zeabur account starts on the **Free** plan — ideal for trying the Zeabur deployment flow for the first time.

The Free plan includes:

- Up to **1 bound external server**
- Build CI at **2C4G**
- **48 hours** of log retention
- Single-file container file management
- Basic database view
- Community support

No credit card required. When you want AI Agent, backups, longer log retention, or a custom domain, upgrade to a paid plan.

## Dev Plan

The **Dev** plan ($5 / month) is built for solo developers who want AI Agent, database backups, and domain management.

Everything in Free, plus:

- Up to **3 bound external servers**
- **7 days** of log retention
- **Zeabur AI** (AI Agent)
- Purchase domains through Zeabur
- Email system (send transactional emails)
- Higher upload limits

## Pro Plan

The **Pro** plan ($19 / month) is for developers who need higher build specs, long log retention, and stronger observability.

Everything in Dev, plus:

- Up to **10 bound external servers**
- Build CI at **4C8G**
- **30 days** of log retention
- Advanced log search
- Log forwarding (log drain)
- High-availability service deployment
- Domain & IP access control
- Custom monitoring metrics & alerts

## Team Plan

The **Team** plan ($79 / month, 3 seats included; **$24 per additional seat / month**) is for small teams that need collaboration, unlimited server binding, and priority support.

Everything in Pro, plus:

- **Unlimited** bound external servers
- **90 days** of log retention
- **3 seats included** (each seat has Pro-level features)
- Team project collaboration
- Role & permission management
- Priority customer & technical support
- Enterprise procurement & invoicing

Supports credit card and Alipay. If you need other payment methods, please contact us.

<Callout type="info">
The **Team** plan does **not** include a free trial. Only **Dev** and **Pro** are eligible for the 14-day free trial.
</Callout>

## Enterprise Plan

**Enterprise** is for companies with compliance needs, custom contracts, and dedicated resources.

Everything in Team, plus:

- Custom usage limits and tailored contracts
- Dedicated technical resources
- Priority support
- Enterprise procurement & invoicing

If the Team plan does not meet your needs, please [contact us](mailto:contact@zeabur.com) for a plan tailored to your scenario.

## 14-Day Free Trial

Every user is entitled to **one** free trial and can choose either the **Dev** ($5/mo) or **Pro** ($19/mo) plan:

- During the trial you get **exactly the same features and quotas** as a paid subscription.
- No subscription fees are charged during the 14-day trial.
- After the trial ends, your chosen plan automatically renews and is charged.
- You can end the trial early — you'll be charged immediately for that plan and a new one-month billing cycle starts that day.
- The **Team** plan does not include a free trial.

## FAQ

### Do I need a paid plan if I already have a dedicated server?

You can use a dedicated server on the Free plan, but a paid plan (Dev / Pro / Team) unlocks additional platform capabilities such as backups, AI Agent, higher quotas, and priority support. Free users can still receive community support on the [Zeabur forum](https://zeabur.com/forum) with no guaranteed response time. See [Community & Support](/community/forum) for details.

### What happens to my services after downgrading?

Your deployed services and existing resources (domains, bound external servers, etc.) are **not deleted** — you can still manage them. However, adding new resources is capped by your new plan's quota. For example, if you had 5 external servers bound on Pro and downgrade to Dev (max 3), the 5 remain manageable but you cannot bind new servers until you upgrade again.

### What happens if I cancel and re-subscribe within the same billing period?

Canceling and re-subscribing creates a new subscription and charges you again. The previous subscription's remaining period is not carried over. If you were charged twice within the same period, please submit a request on the [Zeabur forum](https://zeabur.com/forum) for a refund of the duplicate charge.
