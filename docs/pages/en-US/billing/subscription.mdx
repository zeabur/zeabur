# Subscription

## What is Subscription?

Zeabur offers five plans: **Free**, **Dev**, **Pro**, **Team**, and **Enterprise**. See the [plans](/billing/plans) page for detailed features and use cases.

If you haven't subscribed to any plan, your account defaults to **Free**.

## 14-Day Free Trial

Every user can start **one** 14-day free trial on either the **Dev** or **Pro** plan.

- You get the same features and quotas as a paid subscription during the trial.
- No subscription fees are charged during the 14-day trial.
- After the trial ends, your chosen plan automatically renews and is charged.
- You can end the trial early at any time — the plan is charged immediately and a new one-month billing cycle starts that day.
- The **Team** plan does not include a free trial.

## How to Subscribe

Zeabur's subscription is processed via [Stripe](https://stripe.com/). There are two ways to pay: **credit card** and **prepaid credits**.

### Subscribe with Credit Card

You can subscribe to a plan with your credit card, paying in USD.

- **Setup without subscription**: Go to the [billing page](https://dash.zeabur.com/account/billing) and add your credit card in the **Payment Method** section. Click **Add** to save it, then click **Subscribe** on the [plan page](https://zeabur.com/pricing#upgrade) to activate your subscription.
- **Setup with subscription**: Go to the [plan page](https://zeabur.com/pricing#upgrade) in the dashboard, choose a plan, input your credit card information, and click **Subscribe**. You'll be redirected to Stripe's checkout page to complete the flow.

### Subscribe with Prepaid Credits

If you don't have a credit card that's compatible with Stripe, you can buy prepaid credits first.

Go to the [billing page](https://dash.zeabur.com/account/billing) in the dashboard, click the **Get More Credits** button to top up, then head to the [plan page](https://zeabur.com/pricing#upgrade) to upgrade using those credits.

![Get More Credits](/billing/subscription/get-more-credits.png)

<Callout type="info">
In China, you can subscribe with **Alipay** in addition to credit card.
</Callout>

## Upgrade & Downgrade

- **Upgrades take effect immediately.** You'll be charged a prorated difference between the old and new plans based on the remaining days of the current billing cycle.
- **Downgrades take effect at the end of the current billing cycle.** No refunds are issued and you keep your current features until the cycle ends.

After downgrading, your deployed services and existing resources are **not deleted** — but adding new resources is capped by the new plan's quota.

## How to Change Payment Method

If your card is declined or you want to change your payment method, go to the [billing page](https://dash.zeabur.com/account/billing).

Scroll down to **Unbind Payment Method** to remove the current credit card, then add a new one.

## How to Cancel / Downgrade to Free

If you've subscribed to a paid plan, you can cancel or downgrade to **Free** at any time.

Go to the [billing page in your dashboard](https://dash.zeabur.com/account/billing) and scroll down to the danger zone to unsubscribe.

![Unsubscribe Plan](/billing/subscription/unsubscribe.png)

<Callout>
After cancelling, you can still use all features of your current plan until the end of the current billing cycle. The account automatically switches to the Free plan afterward, and services that exceed Free limits may be suspended.
</Callout>

## Invoices and Receipts

When you make a payment, you'll receive an email with your invoice and receipt attached. You can also view your billing history and download past invoices on the [billing page](https://dash.zeabur.com/account/billing).

- Customized billing information (company name, tax ID, address) requires the **Dev** plan or above.
- Formal invoices with **enterprise procurement & invoicing** are available on the **Team** plan and above.
