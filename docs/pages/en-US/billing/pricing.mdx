# Pricing

Zeabur offers four subscription tiers plus a custom Enterprise option. Each tier bundles platform features and resource limits — see [Pricing](/pricing) for the full plan list.

## Subscription Tiers

- **[Free Plan](/pricing/free-plan)** — US$0/month
- **[Dev Plan](/pricing/dev-plan)** — US$5/month (14-day free trial)
- **[Pro Plan](/pricing/pro-plan)** — US$19/month (14-day free trial)
- **[Team Plan](/pricing/team-plan)** — US$79/month for 3 seats (+US$24/seat)
- **Enterprise** — [contact us](https://zeabur.com/forum)

For the full feature comparison, visit [zeabur.com/pricing](https://zeabur.com/pricing).

## Refunds

For any refund issues, please contact us on [our community forum](https://zeabur.com/forum).

For the **Dev Plan**, **Pro Plan**, or **Team Plan**, you can request a refund for the remaining balance if you cancel your subscription. For example, if you cancel after using a plan for 10 days in a 30-day cycle, you can receive a refund for the remaining 20 days. If you request a refund within 24 hours of the most recent charge, you will receive a full refund.

For **Server** users, Zeabur no longer supports refunds for servers created or renewed after July 21, 2025. To avoid being charged in the upcoming billing cycle, you can disable the auto-renewal settings.

> Please be noted, this only affects the servers created or renewed after July 21, 2025.

![disable auto renewal](/billing/pricing/auto-renewal.png)

You can choose to receive your refund as Zeabur credits or back to your original payment method. If you opt for Zeabur credits, the refund is usually processed instantly after approval. If you choose to refund to your original payment method, it may take up to 7 days to process, and you will need to pay the Stripe transaction fee, which is approximately 30% of the amount refunded.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is the relationship between VPS and subscription plans?

VPS is the server you purchase from cloud providers at discounted prices through Zeabur. Subscription plans provide additional management services on top of your VPS, including a unified dashboard, monitoring, backups, AI Agent, and technical support.

### Why should I buy VPS through Zeabur?

Zeabur offers exclusive discounted pricing from top cloud providers, plus a unified management platform. You get lower prices, instant setup, built-in monitoring, automatic backups, and priority support — all in one place.

### Which subscription plan is right for me?

The Free Plan is great for getting started with basic dashboard access. The Dev Plan is ideal for individual developers who need full monitoring, backups, and AI Agent. The Pro Plan adds log forwarding, larger quotas, and priority support — recommended for production workloads. The Team Plan is designed for teams managing multiple servers with role and permission management.

### Can I use Zeabur without a credit card?

Yes. You can purchase prepaid credits without a credit card. Alipay is also available as an alternative payment method. See the [subscription page](/billing/subscription) for details.

### Will I get a receipt?

When you make a payment, you will receive an email with your invoice and receipt attached. You can also find your billing history on the [billing page](https://zeabur.com/account/billing).

If you need customized billing information on your invoices (e.g., company name, tax ID, or address), you must subscribe to the **Dev Plan** or above. If you require a formal invoice, you must subscribe to the **Team Plan**.

### Which cloud providers are supported?

We currently support AWS (Lightsail), Hetzner, DigitalOcean, Tencent Cloud, Linode, Alibaba Cloud, and Google Cloud (GCP). We are continuously adding more providers.
