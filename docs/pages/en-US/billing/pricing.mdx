# Pricing

Zeabur offers a consumption-based pricing model for your projects. You won't get any bills for resources you don't use.

Instead, Zeabur charges by the minute for each resource that your services actually use.

## Pricing Rates

<Callout type="warning">
  Zeabur is phasing out shared clusters. New projects can no longer be created on shared clusters, and existing shared cluster projects will stop accepting new services from April 1, 2026. Existing services will continue to run. For more details, see the [changelog](https://zeabur.com/changelogs/phasing-out-shared-cluster).
</Callout>

There are two kinds of servers in Zeabur: _shared clusters_ and [_dedicated servers_](/dedicated-server). These pricing rates apply only to **shared clusters**, and we do not charge a usage fee for dedicated servers.

Similarly, the Developer Plan's US$5 monthly free quota applies only to shared cluster usage and cannot be used toward dedicated server rental fees.

The rates for usage in the **shared clusters** are as the following:

- vCPU - No cost ($0.00 / vCPU-minute)

- Memory - **US$0.00025** / GB-minute (approx. US$10.80 / GB-month)
  - You will only be charged for the memory your services actually use. If your service crashes or is suspended, you won't incur any memory usage charges.

- Network Egress - **US$0.10** / GB (US$10.00 / 100GB)
  - 1GB accommodates about 1,000 unique visitors accessing your 1MiB page without a CDN or compression.
  - [Private networking](/networking/private) does not count as egress bandwidth.

- Persistent Volume - **US$0.20** / GB-month

## Refunds

For any refund issues, please contact us on [our community forum](https://zeabur.com/forum).

For the **Developer Plan** or **Team Plan**, you can request a refund for the remaining balance if you cancel your subscription. For example, if you cancel your Developer Plan after using it for 10 days, you can receive a refund for the remaining 20 days. If you request a refund within 24 hours, you will receive a full refund.

For **Dedicated Server** users, Zeabur no longer supports refunds for servers created or renewed after July 21, 2025. To avoid being charged in the upcoming billing cycle, you can disable the auto-renewal settings.

> Please be noted, this only affects the servers created or renewed after July 21, 2025.

![disable auto renewal](/billing/pricing/auto-renewal.png)

You can choose to receive your refund as Zeabur credits or back to your original payment method. If you opt for Zeabur credits, the refund is usually processed instantly after approval. If you choose to refund to your original payment method, it may take up to 7 days to process, and you will need to pay the Stripe transaction fee, which is approximately 30% of the amount refunded.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Which subscription plan is right for me?

| Plan | Monthly | Who it's for |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Free** | $0 | Users trying out the Zeabur deployment flow for the first time |
| **Dev** | $5 | Solo developers who need AI Agent, database backups, and domain management |
| **Pro** | $19 | Developers who need higher build specs, long log retention, and stronger observability |
| **Team** | $79 | Small teams that need collaboration, unlimited server binding, and priority support |
| **Enterprise** | Contact us | Companies with compliance needs, custom contracts, and dedicated resources |

See the [Plans](/billing/plans) page for a detailed breakdown.

### What is the 14-day free trial?

Every user is entitled to one free trial and can choose either the **Dev** ($5/mo) or **Pro** ($19/mo) plan. During the trial you get exactly the same features and quotas as a paid subscription. The **Team** plan does not include a free trial.

No subscription fees are charged during the 14-day trial. After the trial ends, your chosen plan automatically renews and is charged. You can cancel before the trial ends to avoid being charged.

### What is the relationship between VPS and subscription plans?

VPS is the server you purchase from cloud providers at discounted prices through Zeabur. Subscription plans provide additional management services on top of your VPS, including a unified dashboard, monitoring, backups, AI Agent, and technical support.

### Why should I buy VPS through Zeabur?

Zeabur offers exclusive discounted pricing from top cloud providers, plus a unified management platform. You get lower prices, instant setup, built-in monitoring, automatic backups, and priority support — all in one place.

### Can I use Zeabur without a credit card?

Yes. You can purchase prepaid credits without a credit card. In China, **Alipay** is also available as a payment method. See the [subscription page](/billing/subscription) for details.

### Will I get a receipt?

When you make a payment, you'll receive an email with your invoice and receipt attached. You can also find your billing history on the [billing page](https://dash.zeabur.com/account/billing).

- Customized billing information (company name, tax ID, address) requires the **Dev** plan or above.
- Formal invoices with enterprise procurement & invoicing are available on the **Team** plan and above.

### Which cloud providers are supported?

We currently support AWS (Lightsail), Hetzner, DigitalOcean, Tencent Cloud, Linode, Alibaba Cloud, and Google Cloud (GCP). We are continuously adding more providers.
