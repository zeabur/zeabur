# Rewards & Referrals

Zeabur offers multiple reward programs so you can earn benefits while using and promoting the platform.

- [Referral Program](/rewards/referral) — Share your referral code to give friends discounts and earn commissions
- [Partner Program](/rewards/partner) — Promote Zeabur as an instructor or consultant and earn partnership rewards
- [Contribution Reward Program](/rewards/contribution) — Contribute templates or translations for free usage credits
- [Open Source Sponsorship](/rewards/sponsor) — Apply for Zeabur sponsorship for your open-source project
- [Redeem Prepaid Card](/rewards/redeem-card) — Redeem a prepaid card to your account balance
