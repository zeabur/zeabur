# Register a Domain


<Callout type="info">
  Domain purchase requires a **Developer** or **Team** plan. Free-plan users will be prompted to upgrade.
</Callout>

## Searching for a Domain

Open the **Domains** section from the sidebar and enter the domain name you want. Zeabur checks availability across 9 TLDs (`.com`, `.net`, `.org`, `.io`, `.dev`, `.app`, `.co`, `.me`, `.xyz`) simultaneously.

Results load progressively — popular TLDs (`.com`, `.dev`, `.app`, `.io`) appear first, followed by the rest. Pricing is displayed in real-time next to each result.

If you type a full domain like `example.com`, a **Best Match** card appears at the top with the exact result.

## Purchasing a Domain

<Steps>

### Select a Domain

Find an available domain from the search results and click **Add to Cart**.

### Choose a Registrant Profile

In the purchase dialog, select an existing [registrant profile](/domains/profiles) or create a new one. If you have a default profile set, it will be pre-selected.

### Complete Checkout

Click **Next** to proceed to the Zeabur checkout page. The checkout flow is the same as other Zeabur purchases — your existing payment method on file will be used.

After payment, the domain enters **Pending** status while registration is being processed. Most domains become **Active** within a few minutes.

</Steps>

## Registrant Verification

Some TLDs require the registrant to verify their email address after purchase. If verification is required:

1. A verification email is sent to the email address in your registrant profile.
2. The domain card in the management page shows a **Verification Pending** badge (a visual indicator shown on Active domains).
3. Click the badge or go to the domain detail page to resend the verification email if needed.

<Callout type="warning">
  Domains that remain unverified may be suspended by the registry. Complete verification promptly after purchase.
</Callout>
