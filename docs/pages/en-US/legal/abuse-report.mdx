# Abuse Reporting Guidelines

Zeabur works with cybersecurity organizations to combat crime and malicious use. We encourage you to report any behavior that violates the [Fair Use Guidelines](/legal/fair-use-guideline). If you find any website or activity that violates the [Fair Use Guidelines](/legal/fair-use-guideline), please report it to us immediately.

If you are a government agency or a cybersecurity organization, you can also contact us to integrate with an automated reporting system.

## Contact

We provide the following contact methods:

- Email: [contact@zeabur.com](mailto:contact@zeabur.com)
- Discord community: [Zeabur Discord](https://zeabur.com/dc)

We will respond to your report within 2 business days and let you know the handling status. If you do not hear back within 2 business days, please contact us again.
