# Invoicing

Zeabur provides invoicing for [Team Plan](/pricing/team-plan) and above, making it easy for businesses and organizations to handle accounting.

<Callout type="info">
Invoicing is only available for **Team Plan** and above. [Dev Plan](/pricing/dev-plan) and Free Plan users who need invoices should upgrade to the Team Plan first.
</Callout>

## How to Request an Invoice

1. Go to the [Dashboard billing page](https://dash.zeabur.com/account/billing).
2. In the invoice settings section, fill in your company name and Tax ID.
3. After saving, future bills will automatically include your invoice information.

## Tax ID Configuration

In the invoice settings on the billing page, you can configure:

- **Company name** — The full company or organization name displayed on the invoice.
- **Tax ID** — Your tax identification number (e.g., Taiwan 統一編號, VAT number, or other regional tax IDs).
- **Billing address** — Your company's billing address.

## Supported Countries / Regions

Zeabur provides invoicing through Stripe, supporting tax identification numbers for the following countries and regions:

- **Taiwan** — 統一編號 (Tax ID)
- **United States** — EIN (Employer Identification Number)
- **European Union** — VAT Number
- **Japan** — 法人番号 (Corporate Number)
- **Other Stripe-supported countries/regions**

If your country/region is not listed above, please reach out on our [community forum](https://zeabur.com/forum) and we'll help you set it up.

## Invoice Contents

Zeabur invoices include the following:

- Billing period
- Subscription plan fees
- Resource usage breakdown (memory, network, storage, etc.)
- Server fees (if applicable)
- Taxes (based on local regulations)

## FAQ

### Can I get invoices for previous months?

Yes. Please reach out on our [community forum](https://zeabur.com/forum) with the months you need and your account information.

### What currency are invoices in?

Invoices are issued in US Dollars (USD), matching Zeabur's billing currency.

### Can Dev Plan users get invoices?

The Dev Plan does not currently support invoicing. If you need invoices, please upgrade to the [Team Plan](/pricing/team-plan).
