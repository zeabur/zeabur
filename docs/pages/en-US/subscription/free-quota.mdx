# Free Quota

Zeabur provides free credits to all users, allowing you to try the platform without paying.

## How Credits Are Calculated

Free credits are **virtual credits that reset monthly**, used to offset resource usage on shared clusters. The amount varies by plan:

| Plan | Monthly Free Credits |
| --- | --- |
| Serverless (Free Plan) | Limited (varies by account status) |
| Dev Plan | US$5 (included in monthly fee) |
| Team Plan | US$5 (included in monthly fee) |

For paid plans, the "free credits" are the credits included in the monthly fee. For example, the Dev Plan's US$5 monthly fee includes US$5 in credits — if your resource usage stays under US$5, no additional charges apply.

## What's Covered

Free credits **only apply to shared cluster resource usage**, including:

- Memory usage
- Network egress
- Persistent storage

### Not Covered by Free Credits

The following are **not** deducted from free credits and require separate payment:

- **Server fees** — Monthly rental for servers is billed separately.
- **AI Hub credits** — AI Hub uses its own credit system and requires separate top-ups.

## When Credits Run Out

### Serverless (Free Plan)

When monthly free credits are exhausted:

1. All services are **automatically suspended**.
2. You'll receive a notification about the depleted credits.
3. Services resume automatically when credits reset next month.
4. To restore services immediately, upgrade to the [Dev Plan](/pricing/dev-plan) or add credits.

### Paid Plans

When the included monthly credits are exhausted:

1. Overage is automatically charged to your **credit card or prepaid balance**.
2. If the charge fails, services won't be suspended immediately, but you'll receive a payment reminder.
3. Prolonged non-payment may result in service suspension.

</Callout>

## FAQ

### Do unused credits roll over?

No. Free credits reset monthly. Unused credits do not carry over to the next month.

### How do I check my remaining credits?

Go to the [Dashboard billing page](https://dash.zeabur.com/account/billing) to view your current month's usage and remaining credits.
