# Refund Policy

## Unsubscribing from a Subscription Plan

For **Developer Plan (Dev Plan)**, **Pro Plan**, and **Team Plan**:

- After cancelling your subscription, you can continue using the current plan's features until the end of the current billing cycle. After the cycle ends, your account will automatically downgrade to the Free Plan.

### How to Unsubscribe

<Steps>

### Go to the Dashboard billing page

Open the [Dashboard billing page](https://dash.zeabur.com/account/billing).

### Find the "**Unsubscribe**" button

Scroll down to the subscription section.

### Confirm the cancellation

Review the confirmation prompt and complete the cancellation.

</Steps>

![Unsubscribe](/billing/subscription/unsubscribe.png)

<Callout type="warning">
  After downgrading to the Free Plan, your currently deployed containerized
  services will be automatically suspended by the system. Make sure to back up
  important data before cancelling.
</Callout>

## Server Refunds

- **First refund**: Self-service cancellation on the Server page. Unconditional refund to **Zeabur Credits**, no review required.
- **Second and subsequent refunds**: Submit a refund request on the [Zeabur Forum](https://zeabur.com/forum) for review by customer support.

### Refund Notes

- To avoid charges in the next billing cycle, disable auto-renewal in advance.

![Disable auto-renewal](/billing/pricing/auto-renewal.png)

Server refund processing time and fees depend on where the refund is issued:

| Refund Method               | Processing Time        | Fees                                           |
| --------------------------- | ---------------------- | ---------------------------------------------- |
| **Zeabur Credits**          | Instant after approval | None                                           |
| **Original payment method** | Up to 7 business days  | ~30% of refund amount (Stripe transaction fee) |

<Callout type="info">
  Choosing Zeabur Credits avoids the Stripe processing fee and is faster.
  Credits can be used for future subscriptions or resource usage.
</Callout>

<Callout type="warning">
  Before requesting a refund, make sure all data on the server has been backed
  up or confirmed as no longer needed — **data cannot be recovered after the
  server is destroyed**.
</Callout>

## Prepaid Credits Refund

Prepaid credits are currently **non-refundable**. Please add credits according to your actual needs.
