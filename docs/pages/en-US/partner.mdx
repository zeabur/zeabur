# Partner Program

The Zeabur Partner Program offers collaboration opportunities for instructors, event organizers, enterprise consultants, and system integrators to earn rewards by promoting or onboarding Zeabur.

## Two Partnership Models

### Education Partner

For **instructors, community leaders, hackathon organizers, and educational institutions**. Promote Zeabur through teaching and technical events using Event Codes that give attendees free trial access, and earn rewards based on retained paying users.

- Receive a dedicated Event Code for attendees
- Retention count and reward payout one month after the event
- Ideal for workshops, online courses, hackathons, and community events

[Learn more →](/partner/event-partner)

### Sales Partner

For **enterprise consultants, system integrators, and technology consulting firms**. Help enterprise clients or institutions adopt Zeabur and earn recurring commissions based on the stages you participate in.

- Ideal for large contracts, institutional procurement, and technical consulting
- Two-layer commission: per-deal tiered rate × contribution stage weighting
- Specific rates, thresholds, and terms are individually negotiated in each contract — not publicly disclosed

[Learn more →](/partner/sales-partner)

## How It Differs from the Referral Program

Zeabur also offers a [Referral Program](/rewards/referral) that lets any user earn rewards through referral codes. Key differences:

| Aspect | Referral Program | Partner Program |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Who | Any user | Instructors / Organizers / Consultants / Integrators |
| How | Automatic referral code flow | Manual review and contract |
| Rewards | Referral code commissions (by tier) | Event retention rewards / Sales Partner commissions |
| Terms | Unified platform terms | Individually negotiated per contract |

<Callout type="info">
A single transaction can only go through the Referral Program or the Partner Program — **they do not stack**.
</Callout>

## How to Apply

Sign up at [partner.zeabur.com](https://partner.zeabur.com) and submit an application based on your partnership type:

- **Education Partner** — Provide event name, expected attendance, dates, and format.
- **Sales Partner** — Provide company information, expected client types, and engagement scale.

The Zeabur team will reach out after receiving your application to discuss terms.
