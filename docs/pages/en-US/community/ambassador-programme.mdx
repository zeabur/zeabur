# Zeabur Ambassador Programme

Come help shape the Zeabur community with us.

Ambassadors are the partners who bring Zeabur into the community — every deployment you ship, every story you share, every event you host carries the product to more developers.

## What You'll Do

The format is open. Here's what people in the community typically do:

* **Content** — Write articles, make videos, record podcasts about what you build on Zeabur
* **Events** — Host meetups, workshops, or hackathons in your city
* **Deep Use** — Use Zeabur deeply, report issues, share real-world cases
* **Templates** — Contribute high-quality templates to the marketplace and community

## Ambassador Benefits

* More from the product
  * Pro plan access
  * $30 USD/month in Zeabur credits (covers the Pro plan)
  * Early access to unreleased and beta features
* Closer access to the team
  * Ambassador-only channel — direct line to the Zeabur team
  * AMAs — direct conversation with the Zeabur team
  * Developer council — your feedback shapes the product roadmap
* More brand resources
  * Event sponsorship
  * For large-scale or high-impact use, we'll talk 1:1 about deeper collaboration

## Who We're Looking For

No fixed profile required — but if you're one of these developers, you'll fit naturally:

* **Builders / Creators** — You deploy your own work on Zeabur and document the process
* **Community Leaders** — You're already a node in some language scene, Discord, Forum, or local event
* **Power Users** — You use Zeabur as production infrastructure, deeply

Our requirements: deployment activity in the past 90 days, verifiable identity, agreement to the [Code of Conduct](/en-US/community/code-of-conduct).

## What We Expect From You

When credits are part of the deal, there's a minimum bar — but the format is open and we don't set KPIs.

At least one publicly verifiable contribution every six months. Any of the following counts:

* One article, video, or podcast
* One meetup, workshop, or talk
* One published template
* One public deployment case study
* Five or more high-quality, in-depth answers on Forum / Discord

## FAQ

**How does the Ambassador Programme relate to the referral program?**

They're decoupled — the referral program is open to all Zeabur users on equal terms and isn't an Ambassador perk. High-impact Ambassadors who drive significant referrals can negotiate case-by-case deeper terms under our extended collaboration framework.

**What if I can't make every AMA?**

No problem — AMAs are recorded and available afterward. Attendance is encouraged but not required.

**What happens if I don't contribute in six months?**

If your activity and engagement fall short of expectations, your ambassador benefits pause.

**Can I nominate friends?**

Yes. During the whitelist phase, Zeabur invites directly; once we open up, public applications will be available.

## Apply

The Ambassador Programme is currently whitelist-based; Zeabur invites the founding team directly. Once we open public applications, the entry point will appear here.
