# **Get Help**

Sometimes you may encounter some problems when using Zeabur. You can get help from Zeabur team in our [Zeabur Forum](https://zeabur.com/forum).

![Zeabur Forum](/community/get-help/Zeabur-Forum.png)

## **How to Get Help**

In our Forum, we have a button called `New Post` for everyone to create the issue ticket.

![Zeabur Forum Overview](/community/get-help/zeabur-forum-overview.gif)

### **Post Format**

Following the format to describe your problem more clearly can help us target and solve your problem faster.

1. Select Which **Issue Type** 
2. Describe your problem shortly in the **Title**.
3. Choose either **Project** or **Server** - We will list out all the Project / Server in your Zeabur Account
4. Inside the services, choose which services has the problem 
5. Then Post the Error Messages from logs.
6. Last, Add the content context to help our support team understand the whole landscape of the problem.

![Ticket System](/community/get-help/Ticket-System.png)

This will create a private post where only the staff will be able to see your phone number.

### Refund Requests

Fill out the refund request template so we are able to assist with the least amount of friction and back and forth.

![refund requests page](/community/get-help/refund-requests.png)

## Misc.

![Others](/community/get-help/other-issues.png)

Select Others tab and try to be as descriptive as possible for most effective from the Zeabur Team

### **Priority Support**

- Team Plan: Response within half a business day
- Dev Plan: Response within 3 business days
- Free Plan: Response within 5 business days

**Other Ways to Contact Us**

While Zeabur Forum is our official support platform, you can also reach out to us via email for support or inquiries:

- **Email:** [contact@zeabur.com](mailto:contact@zeabur.com)

For faster response times and access to our community resources, we recommend using Discord. However, if you prefer email communication, feel free to send us a message and we’ll get back to you as soon as possible.
