# Plans

Zeabur offers four subscription plans plus a custom Enterprise option:

- **[Free Plan](/pricing/free-plan)** — US$0/month, for personal exploration.
- **[Dev Plan](/pricing/dev-plan)** — US$5/month, 14-day free trial. For individual developers.
- **[Pro Plan](/pricing/pro-plan)** — US$19/month, 14-day free trial. Recommended for production.
- **[Team Plan](/pricing/team-plan)** — US$79/month for 3 seats, +US$24/seat. For teams.
- **Enterprise Plan** — [contact us](mailto:contact@zeabur.com) for a plan tailored to your usage scenario.

For the full feature comparison and refund policy, see [Pricing Plans](/pricing/pricing-plans). For the latest pricing, visit [zeabur.com/pricing](https://zeabur.com/pricing).
