# Pro Plan

The Pro Plan is Zeabur's recommended tier for production workloads — **US$19/month** with a **14-day free trial**. It builds on the Dev Plan with **log forwarding**, **larger CI specs**, **longer log retention**, **higher Email/Domain/API Key/Webhook quotas**, **custom service icons**, and **priority support**.

For the full feature list and the latest quotas, see the [comparison chart](/pricing).

## Use Cases

- **Small production deployments** — Services that benefit from longer log retention, larger CI builds, and direct support channels.
- **Apps with higher transactional email volume** — The Pro Plan's monthly Email quota covers most early-stage SaaS and e-commerce apps.
- **Operations-heavy workloads** — Log forwarding to your existing observability stack (Datadog, Grafana, etc.) keeps Pro-tier services aligned with your central monitoring.
- **Frontend-heavy apps with many domains** — Pro's domain and API key headroom covers most multi-environment setups.

## When to Upgrade to Team

If you need **multiple members collaborating on the same projects**, **role and permission management**, **invoicing / tax ID support**, or **unlimited external server bindings**, the [Team Plan](/pricing/team-plan) (US$79/month for 3 seats, +US$24/seat) is the next step.

<Callout type="info">
For larger-scale custom plans, dedicated support, and SLAs, check out the **Enterprise Plan** — [contact us](https://zeabur.com/forum) for details.
</Callout>

## How to Subscribe

Go to [zeabur.com/pricing](https://zeabur.com/pricing) and select Pro Plan, or upgrade from the [Dashboard billing page](https://dash.zeabur.com/account/billing). Credit card and Alipay are supported — see [Payment Methods](/subscription/payment-methods) for details.
