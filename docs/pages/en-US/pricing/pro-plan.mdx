# Pro Plan

The Pro Plan is Zeabur's recommended tier for production workloads — **US$19/month** with a **14-day free trial**. It builds on the Dev Plan with **log forwarding**, **larger CI specs**, **longer log retention**, **higher Email/Domain/API Key quotas**, **custom service icons**, and **priority support**.

## Use Cases

- **Small production deployments** — Services that benefit from longer log retention, larger CI builds, and direct support channels.
- **Apps with higher transactional email volume** — The 50,000 emails/month quota covers most early-stage SaaS and e-commerce apps.
- **Operations-heavy workloads** — Log forwarding to your existing observability stack (Datadog, Grafana, etc.) keeps Pro-tier services aligned with your central monitoring.
- **Frontend-heavy apps with many domains** — 10 domain slots and 100 API keys give Pro plenty of headroom.

## When to Upgrade to Team

If you need **multiple members collaborating on the same projects**, **role and permission management**, **invoicing / tax ID support**, or **unlimited external server bindings**, the [Team Plan](/pricing/team-plan) (US$79/mo for 3 seats, +US$24/seat) is the next step.
