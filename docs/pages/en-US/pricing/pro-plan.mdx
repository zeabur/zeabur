# Pro Plan

The Pro Plan is Zeabur's plan for developers who need **higher build specs, long log retention, and stronger observability**. At **US$19/month**, it layers production-grade capabilities on top of the Dev Plan.

<Callout type="info">
Eligible for the **14-day free trial** — same features as paid, no charge until the trial ends. See [14-Day Free Trial](/billing/subscription#14-day-free-trial).
</Callout>

## What's included (vs. Dev)

Everything in Dev, plus:

- Up to **10 bound external servers** (Dev: 3)
- **Build CI at 4C8G** (Dev: 2C4G) — faster, more powerful builds
- **30 days** of log retention (Dev: 7 days)
- **Advanced log search** — filter and query historical logs
- **Log forwarding (log drain)** — stream logs to third-party systems
- **High-availability service deployment**
- **Domain & IP access control** — restrict who can reach your services
- Higher file upload limits

## Use Cases

- **Production services with traffic** — Higher CI, HA deployment, and log retention keep your production stack healthy.
- **Teams of one that need observability** — Advanced log search, log drain, and custom alerts for debugging at scale.
- **Security-sensitive services** — Domain & IP access control locks down who can reach your endpoints.
- **Long-running builds** — 4C8G build CI ships faster, especially for monorepos.
