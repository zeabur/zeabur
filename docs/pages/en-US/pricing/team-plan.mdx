# Team Plan

The Team Plan is Zeabur's collaborative plan built for small teams that need **collaboration, unlimited server binding, and priority support**. It starts at **US$79/month** with 3 seats included, and each additional seat costs **US$24/month**.

<Callout type="warning">
The Team plan does **not** include a free trial. Start a trial on Dev or Pro if you'd like to evaluate the platform first.
</Callout>

### Seat Model

Team Plan supports multi-member collaboration. Every seat has Pro-level features for that member. The base subscription includes **3 seats**; additional seats are **US$24 per seat per month**.

Example: a team of 5 pays `$79 + 2 × $24 = $127 / month`.

## Seat Roles & Permissions

Team Plan seats come in three roles: **Viewer**, **Editor**, and **Admin**. Assign roles based on what each member needs to do — read-only visibility, day-to-day development work, or full administrative control.

- **Viewer** — read-only access across the team's resources. Best for stakeholders, auditors, or new hires during onboarding.
- **Editor** _(Coming soon)_ — can build and ship: create/modify projects, trigger deploys, rollback, search and download logs. Cannot delete resources or manage members/billing.
- **Admin** — full administrative control, including member management, destructive operations, and billing. Required for any action that affects the team itself or its subscription.

## What's included (vs. Pro)

Everything in Pro, plus:

- **Unlimited** bound external servers (Pro: 10)
- **90 days** of log retention (Pro: 30 days)
- **3 seats included** (each at Pro level)
- **Team project collaboration**
- **Role & permission management**
- **Priority customer & technical support**
- **Batch file operations** on containers
- **Enterprise procurement & invoicing**

## Use Cases

- **Startup teams** — Multiple developers jointly managing deployment environments.
