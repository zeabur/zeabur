# Redeem Prepaid Card

Zeabur prepaid cards (Vouchers) are sold or gifted by third-party partners and can be redeemed to your Zeabur account balance. Your balance can then be used to upgrade to a paid plan, purchase dedicated servers, or pay for shared cluster usage.

<Callout type="info">
If you received an **Event Code** — for example from attending a workshop, hackathon, or instructor-led course — see the [Redeem Event Code](/partner/event-partner/redeem-event-code) instead. Event Codes and prepaid cards are different mechanisms.
</Callout>

## How to Redeem

<Steps>

### Log in to Zeabur

Log in at [zeabur.com/login](https://zeabur.com/login), or [create an account](https://zeabur.com/login) if you don't have one.

### Go to Billing

Click your avatar in the top-right and choose "Billing", or go directly to [zeabur.com/account/billing](https://zeabur.com/account/billing).

### Click "Redeem Credits"

Find the "Redeem Credits" button in the upper-right corner of the "Account Balance" section and click it.

### Enter the Card Code

Enter your prepaid card code in the dialog, then click "Redeem". On success, you'll see a notification in the bottom-right corner and your account balance will update immediately.

</Steps>

## If You Encounter Problems

- **Expired prepaid card** — Confirm the validity period with the issuer. Expired cards cannot be recovered.
- **Invalid code** — Double-check for typos and correct capitalization.
- **Other issues** — Contact customer support on the [Zeabur community forum](https://zeabur.com/forum).
