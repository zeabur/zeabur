# Sales Partner

The Sales Partner program is for **enterprise consultants, system integrators, and technology consulting firms**. When you help enterprise clients, institutions, or organizations adopt Zeabur, you earn recurring commissions based on the stages you actually participate in.

## When It Applies

The Sales Partner program applies in the following scenarios:

- **Enterprise cloud migration** — Help businesses migrate existing services to Zeabur.
- **Institutional procurement** — Bulk or contract-based purchases by educational institutions, government agencies, or enterprise IT departments, including Vouchers, invoicing, and contract-required cases.
- **Technical consulting** — As a technical consultant or system integrator, incorporate Zeabur into the deployment plans you design for clients.
- **Long-term service partnerships** — Provide deployment, operations, or payment collection services to clients using Zeabur as the underlying platform.

If the amount is below threshold and the user pays individually through self-service, use the [Referral Program](/rewards/referral) automatic flow instead. Specific thresholds and conditions are agreed individually in each contract.

<Callout type="info">
A single transaction can only go through the Referral Program or the Sales Partner path — **they do not stack**.
</Callout>

## Commission Structure

Sales Partner commissions combine two layers, calculated based on your actual involvement.

### Layer 1: Per-Deal Tiered Rate

Each deal is calculated independently using a progressive rate based on deal size. Rates are **individually negotiated** based on partner type, market positioning, and engagement scale — public standard rates are not disclosed.

### Layer 2: Contribution Stage Weighting

Your share of each deal is weighted by the stages you actually own. The four stages sum to 100%. Stages that Zeabur handles internally are not paid out.

| Stage | Definition | Attribution Criteria |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Lead (Introduction) | Find the customer | Timestamp of when you facilitated the first contact between both parties (including creating a dedicated communication group) |
| Close (Contract) | Close the deal | Signed quote from both parties |
| Deliver (Service) | Service delivery | You handle all client-side questions and operational support without requiring the Zeabur engineering team |
| Collect (Payments) | Collect payments | You complete collection and invoicing without Zeabur needing to chase |

### Common Participation Patterns

| Scenario | Lead | Close | Deliver | Collect |
| --- | :---: | :---: | :---: | :---: |
| Full service | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ |
| Introduction only | ✓ |   |   |   |
| Introduction + Service | ✓ |   | ✓ |   |
| Service + Collect |   |   | ✓ | ✓ |
| Operations only (no Lead) |   | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ |
| Introduction + Service + Collect | ✓ |   | ✓ | ✓ |

### Multi-Party Splits

Different stages of the same deal can be owned by different partners. Each deal is calculated in isolation (no cross-pool), and each party settles independently without affecting the others.

## Supporting Rules

- **Settlement currency** — Commissions are settled in USD. Exchange costs are borne by the Sales Partner.
- **Settlement cycle** — Commissions are calculated monthly and posted to the Partner Portal after mutual confirmation.
- **Holding period** — A holding period applies before commissions can be withdrawn. Client churn during the holding period results in automatic deduction; after the holding period, already-withdrawn amounts are not clawed back. Specific duration is set by contract.
- **Minimum sales commitment** — A minimum periodic commitment applies. Failure to meet it over consecutive periods allows Zeabur to adjust rates or terminate the contract. Specific amount is set by contract.
- **Lead attribution** — Lead attribution requires Zeabur confirmation before being locked.
- **Stage locking** — Stage ownership is locked when the deal is registered; changes require written consent from both parties.
- **Commission continuity** — Commissions continue as long as the client keeps paying; they stop when the client churns.

<Callout type="warning">
The Sales Partner program and the Referral Program cannot be applied to the same account simultaneously, to avoid double-benefit conflicts.
</Callout>

## Partnership Process

1. **Contact Zeabur** — Apply at [partner.zeabur.com](https://partner.zeabur.com), or reach out via the [Zeabur community forum](https://zeabur.com/forum), describing your client background and estimated engagement scale.
2. **Negotiate contract** — The Zeabur team evaluates and negotiates specific rates, stage weights, and supporting terms with you. Individual contracts are confidential.
3. **Sign contract** — Once terms are confirmed, sign the contract and begin deal tracking.
4. **Client onboarding** — Help the client evaluate, test, and go live.
5. **Monthly settlement** — Once the client's payment succeeds, monthly commissions are settled per the contract.

## Related Addenda

Some partnerships involve separate contracts or addenda, such as:

- **Payment collection service fees** — If you act as a payment collector, the associated service fees are governed by a separate contract.
- **Education subsidies** — Partnerships involving schools or educational institutions are handled via a separate addendum/MOU.

## How to Apply

- Sign up at [partner.zeabur.com](https://partner.zeabur.com) and submit an application with your company information, client background, and estimated engagement scale.
- You can also reach out via the [Zeabur community forum](https://zeabur.com/forum) to express interest.
