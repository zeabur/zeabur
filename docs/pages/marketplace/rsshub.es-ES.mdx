# Deploy RSSHub Service

[RSSHub](https://rsshub.app) is an open source, easy to use, and extensible RSS feed aggregator, it's capable of generating RSS feeds from pretty much everything.

![RSSHub](/marketplace/rsshub/intro.png)

This article will teach you how to deploy your own RSSHub service on Zeabur through the service market, and bind your own domain name.

## Step 1: Create a project

First, we need to create a project. Go to the [Projects](https://dash.zeabur.com/projects) page, and you will see a list of your projects.

Click the "Create Project" button at the end of the list.

![Create Project](/deploy/common/create-project.en-US.png)

## Step 2: Deploy RSSHub Service

You can deploy RSSHub service in marketplace with one click.

Click on the "Add Service" button or press "cmd + k" in project page, and select "Marketplace".

![Select Marketplace](/deploy/common/select-service.en-US.png)

Find RSSHub in the list and click on it to start deploying.

## Step 3: Bind a domain

In the "Domain" block of the RSSHub service in the Zeabur dashboard, you can bind a domain to it.

This domain can be your own domain or a free domain provided by Zeabur.

For more information about how to bind a domain to your service, please refer to [Bind a domain](../deploy/domain-binding).

## Step 4: Enjoy it

After the deployment is complete, you can access your RSSHub service through the domain name you bound.

Select your favorite RSS feed and subscribe to it in your RSS reader.
