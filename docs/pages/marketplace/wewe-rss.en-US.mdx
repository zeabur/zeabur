# Deploy WeWe RSS on Zeabur

[WeWe RSS](https://github.com/cooderl/wewe-rss) provides an elegant way to subscribe to WeChat Official Accounts as RSS feeds.

This article will show you how to deploy WeWe RSS on Zeabur in a minute.

![demo](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/cooderl/wewe-rss/main/assets/preview1.png)

## Method 1: Deploy from template(Recommended)

We have built a template for you. All you need to do is click the button below, and you will get a running WeWe RSS service.

[![Deploy on Zeabur](https://zeabur.com/button.svg)](https://zeabur.com/templates/DI9BBD)

![Deploy WeWe RSS](/marketplace/wewe-rss/template.png)

## Method 2: Deploy from prebuilt marketplace

### Step 1: Create a project

<CreateProject />

### Step 2: Deploy from marketplace

After creating a project, you will be redirected to the project page.

Click the "Add Service" button and then select "Prebuilt". You will find **WeWe RSS** in the list.

![Deploy](/marketplace/wewe-rss/deploy.png)

Click on it or just press Enter, your WeWe RSS service will start to deploy.

## What's next?

### Bind a domain

While you wait for this service to start running, you can bind a domain to it.

Open the "Networking" tab in the service page and click on "Generate Domain" or "Custom Domain".

For more information about how to bind a domain to your service, please refer to [Bind a domain](../deploy/domain-binding).

### Login and configure WeWe RSS

After binding the domain, you can click the domain to enter the WeWe RSS login page.

![auth](/marketplace/wewe-rss/auth.png)

You will be asked to enter the auth code for the first time. You can get the auth code in variables page, just copy the value of `PASSWORD`.

![login](/marketplace/wewe-rss/copy.png)
