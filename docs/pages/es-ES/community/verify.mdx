# Verify Your Account

Verifying your account links your Zeabur account with the community forum, so you can discuss and engage with the community more easily.

You may also get some credits as a reward for your activity in our community forum.

## How to Get Verified

We use our bot **Zebra** to help us with the verification process.

### Step 1: Join Our Community Forum

First of all, you need to [visit our community forum](https://zeabur.com/forum). You can communicate with our staff and other users there, and post your feedbacks and problems.

### Step 2: Link Your Account

When you get into our community forum, you will see a welcome message from Zebra. You will find a [verify channel](https://discord.com/channels/1060209568820494336/1095916895649153094) in the message content.

![verify](/community/verify/verify.png)

Click on the verify button, you will see an authorization page. Click on the **Authorize** button to link your account with our website.

![authorize](/community/verify/authorize.png)

When you get redirected to our website, you will see a success message in the toaster, and you can close the website.

![verified](/community/verify/verified.png)

## What's Next

After verification, you can participate in discussions on our community forum. When you upgrade to another plan, **Zebra** will automatically give you a special role in our community forum. You can join exclusive channels with that role.

![dev-plan](/community/verify/dev-plan.png)

If you like this format of community interaction, feel free to share your thoughts in our [community forum](https://zeabur.com/forum).
